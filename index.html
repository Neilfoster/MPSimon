MPSimon

The MPSimon game is a take on the Simon game and the iconic MPC drum machine in which players must repeat random sequences of lights and drum samples by pressing the drum pads in the correct order. It's fast-paced play, with lights and sounds that can challenge you.

How To Play

  • Turn the game on by selecting the power button and then pressing start.
  • MPSimon will give the first signal.
  • Repeat the signal by pressing the same pad.
  • MPSIMON will duplicate the first sound and will add one.
  • Repeat these two sounds by pressing the same pads.
  • MPSIMON will duplicate these first two sounds and add one.
  • Continue in this manner as long as you can repeat each sequence of sound correctly.
  • If you cannot repeat the sequence correctly , MPSIMON will repeat the sequence again until you can.
  • Selecting STRICT mode resets the pattern to the beginning if you get the sequence wrong.
  • To win the game repeat the sequence until it reaches 20 consecutive sounds.
